# Information Security Risk Register - ISO 27001 Based Risk Assessment Project

## Overview

This repository contains an Information Security Risk Register developed as part of a Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) learning project. The project simulates how organizations identify, assess, prioritize, and treat information security risks using principles inspired by the ISO 27001 Information Security Management System (ISMS) framework.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate a structured approach to risk management and provide practical exposure to the responsibilities commonly associated with GRC Analysts and Information Security Professionals.

---

## Project Objectives

* Understand the Information Security Risk Management lifecycle.
* Identify critical organizational assets and associated risks.
* Assess threats, vulnerabilities, and potential business impacts.
* Calculate and prioritize risks based on likelihood and impact.
* Recommend appropriate risk treatment strategies.
* Evaluate residual risk after implementing security controls.
* Gain hands-on experience with ISO 27001-inspired risk assessment practices.

---

## Scope of Assessment

The assessment was performed for a fictional organization:

**Organization Name:** Nexus Retail Pvt. Ltd.

The risk register covers several critical business assets, including:

* Customer Databases

* Payment Gateway Integrations

## Risk Assessment Methodology

The project follows a structured risk management approach:

### 1. Asset Identification

Identification of information assets that are critical to business operations.

### 2. Threat Identification

Analysis of potential threats, including:

* Phishing Attacks
* Malware Infections
* Unauthorized Access
* SQL Injection Attacks
* DDoS Attacks
* Credential Misuse
* Hardware Failures
* Data Breaches

### 3. Vulnerability Assessment

Evaluation of weaknesses that may increase the likelihood of successful attacks.

### 4. Risk Analysis

Assessment of:

* Likelihood of occurrence
* Business impact
* Risk score calculation

### 5. Risk Classification

Risks are categorized based on severity:

* High Risk
* Medium Risk
* Low Risk

### 6. Risk Treatment

Appropriate treatment strategies are recommended:

* Risk Mitigation
* Risk Transfer
* Risk Acceptance

### 7. Residual Risk Evaluation

Assessment of remaining risk after implementing recommended controls.

## Security Framework Considerations

The assessment incorporates principles from:

* ISO/IEC 27001
* Information Security Risk Management Practices
* CIA Triad

### CIA Triad Components

#### Confidentiality

Protection of information from unauthorized access.

#### Integrity

Protection against unauthorized modification of information.

#### Availability

Ensuring information and services remain accessible when required.
